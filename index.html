plant note
Welcome to my yard
I will forever be in awe of plants. Forever in awe at the different colours, tastes, textures, sizes and god, just their existance. Forever in awe at ones that are so similar how could they not be the same? Alas they are not.

This project is a small ode to how awesome I think the plants and trees are and how grateful I am for the food on my plate and the ones who grow it. I hope for humanity's sake fresh produce becomes less of a luxury.

Oh veggies,
My veggies.

This project is heavily inspired by flowerseed.io by @nisanord.

Thank you for being here!
Maybe one day you will visit my garden and pluck some of the produce to take home with you.
Pavana
